## Notes on adaptation strategy

- The largest substitution gap is **browser automation parity** (Playwright-level ergonomics). A pragmatic CL port usually keeps either:
  1) direct CDP in CL for core actions, and/or
  2) a small external Playwright helper process for hard cases.
- Channel/provider behavior in the specs is highly compatibility-driven; the CL port should preserve **behavioral adapters** even when the internal architecture changes.
- For maintainability, prefer a thin internal abstraction over HTTP/WS/JSON + per-provider/channel adapters, rather than trying to find one “magic” CL library for all cases.
